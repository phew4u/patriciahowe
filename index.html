About me

My passion to support those who would like to achieve physical health and emotional wellness, grows stronger as I evolve.

"Search within yourself"

Massage therapy is one of the oldest complimentary therapies whose customs and techniques are rooted in ancient history. A therapy that provides both physical and emotional health benefits, in which I trained at School of Bodywork Exeter and gained my qualification as a Bodyworker, and then as a remedial massage therapist.

I feel massage compliments my personal training skills and my 35 + years experience in the fitness industry beautifully.

In the personal training area of my career, I very much love the aspect of supporting people whilst they set and work towards achieving their goals. This can be in 1:1 personal training or in small semi-private groups.

I found it was time to start slowing down my exciting yet hectic journey and shift my focus to the areas that I felt I could be of benefit to people. Sharing both my knowledge and experience, encouraging people to connect/reconnect with themselves both physically and emotionally paving a way for them to feel good holistically.

Crystal therapy has long been a hobby of mine which not only fascinates me but drew me to the field of learning and experiencing more. How are they formed? What are their individual structures? Looking at the metaphysical aspect of crystals, and how they may affect our bodies/lives. I eventually studied and gained an ICGT qualification.

Would you like to improve your physical health, mental wellbeing, and generally just allow time for yourself? Let PHEW be your guiding hand.
